Banana Sour Cream Waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, separated
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 banana, mashed

Steps

  1. Sift together dry ingredients. Set Aside.
  2. Mix together beaten egg yolk, sour cream, milk, butter, and banana. Add to flour mixture, mixing well.
  3. Fold in whipped egg white.
  4. Pour into oiled waffle iron or oiled pan. Cook each side until brown.

Lemon Waffles

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs -- separated
  • 1.4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • fresh lemon juice of 1 entire lemon
  • grated peel of 1 entire lemon
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1-1/4 cups flower
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat egg yolks with the sugar and salt.
  2. Blend in the milk, lemon juice, lemon peel, and butter, beating well.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder.
  4. Beat egg whites until stiff and fold into batter.
  5. As usual, bake in a preheated waffle iron until golden brown and steam no longer escapes iron.
  6. Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh berries, or lemon yogurt.
